Digital Resilience

Exploring the predictors of resilience in youth

Social work Survey Factor analysis

Summary

The concept of resilience, defined as an individual's ability to adapt after being exposed to risk, has been generally assumed to be consistent across environments. We explored whether correlates of resilience differ in IRL versus online settings. We found that youth from gender minorities or with adverse childhood experiences are more likely to develop resilience through social media use, compared to other youth.
