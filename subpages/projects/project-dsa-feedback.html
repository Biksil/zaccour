EU Digital Services Act Feedback

Policy Data access

Summary

At the initiative of colleagues Diyi Liu and Manuel Tonneau, we submitted a feedback statement to the EU Delegated Regulation on data access. The Draft Act aims to specify the conditions under which data sharing between online platforms and researchers should occur, specifically through the establishment of Digital Services Coordinators as mediators for data access requests. Our review of the Act highlights several points of concern, such as under-valuing public interest considerations, implementing data anonymization at the cost of research integrity, and perpetuating unequal access among researchers.
