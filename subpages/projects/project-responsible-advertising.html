Responsible Advertising

Visualizing the evolution of responsible creative advertising.

Data visualization Communication Event

The "20 Years of Great Ads for Good" exhibition, presented at the 2022 Cannes Lions International Festival of Creativity, featured data visualizations retracing the evolutions of responsible creative advertising, derived from a database of over 20,000 ad campaigns curated from 2001 to 2021.

 

The visualizations allowed communication professionals to learn and reflect on the industry's ethical challenges and potential for positive impact.
